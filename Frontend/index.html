What is Stress?

Stress is a physical, mental, or emotional response to external pressures or demands. It is a natural reaction to challenging or threatening situations, but when experienced in excess or for prolonged periods, it can have negative effects on one's health and well-being.

Acute Stress: This is short-term stress that arises from specific events or situations, such as a job interview or a tight deadline. It usually subsides once the situation is resolved.

Chronic Stress: This is long-term stress resulting from ongoing situations, such as financial problems, a demanding job
